Popular Questions

How much can I save on my electricity bills with a solar system?

Savings vary significantly based on your current usage, system size, and local utility rates, but most homeowners save between **50% and 100%** on their monthly electricity bills. We provide a personalized savings projection during your consultation.

What government subsidies and incentives are available?

Homeowners often qualify for the **Federal Solar Investment Tax Credit (ITC)**, which can cover a significant portion of the installation cost. Local and state rebates may also be available, and our team will help you maximize every incentive.

What is the typical payback period for a rooftop solar installation?

The average payback period for a modern solar installation ranges from **6 to 9 years**, after which your electricity generation is essentially free for the remainder of the system's 25+ year lifespan.

How long does the installation process take?

Physical installation usually takes **1 to 3 days**. However, the entire process, including site assessment, design, permitting, and final utility approval, can take **4 to 8 weeks** depending on local regulations.

What maintenance is required after installation?

Modern solar systems require very little maintenance. We recommend occasional cleaning (especially in dusty areas) and an annual professional inspection, which can be covered by our maintenance service packages.
